Cosmetic Teeth Whitening

Everyone has stains on their teeth from coffee, tea, red wine and certain coloured foods. Revive Your Smile performs Cosmetic Teeth Whitening in a very safe and effective way.
Our cosmetic whitening gel contains peroxide and has a water based formula that protects teeth from becoming sensitive and keeping them hydrated. Results are visible in just one session. This procedure is non invasive, and performed by a highly trained professional.

Professional Dental Whitening

This form of Whitening is done with a higher concentration of hydrogen peroxide gel. Prior to the procedure, the first step would be is in depth consultation with the client. Full examination of teeth and gums to determine the right whitening agent suitable for each client.

Dental Hygiene Services

Dental teeth cleaning aims to remove plaque, tarter and stains that have accumulated on the teeth. With regular brushing and flossing, tarter can still remain on teeth.
The professional cleaning of teeth is a very important part of good oral hygiene and is needed to maintain the health of your teeth and gums. Dental hygiene cleaning is also highly recommended before a whitening treatment, it maximizes the benefits from Teeth whitening.

Cosmetic Teeth Whitening Training

Revive Your Smile is offering a Cosmetic Teeth Whitening Training to anyone who wants to become a Teeth Whitening Practitioner. Work at your own schedule. No dental background needed. One day training with in depth theory and hands on experience, taught by a professional. Start your own business by making people smile!
